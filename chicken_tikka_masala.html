Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a rich and creamy curry dish made with marinated chicken pieces cooked in a spiced tomato sauce. This popular dish has its roots in Indian cuisine but has also become a staple in many parts of the world, often served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook the marinated chicken pieces until they are golden and cooked through. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, saut√© chopped onions until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add tomato puree and let it cook for a few minutes. Then add heavy cream and salt.
  5. Add the cooked chicken back into the pan and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with naan or rice.
