Chicken 65

Description

Chicken 65 is a popular, spicy, and flavorful South Indian appetizer. Made with marinated chicken deep-fried to perfection and coated in a mix of flavorful spices, this dish is perfect for any occasion.

The origins of Chicken 65 are debated, but it is widely loved for its unique flavor profile, combining a crispy texture with the boldness of chili, garlic, and curry leaves.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into small cubes
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • 1 tbsp rice flour
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tsp red chili paste (optional)
  • Lemon wedges (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, and yogurt. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Add egg, corn flour, and rice flour to the marinated chicken and mix well.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan and fry the chicken pieces until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  4. In a separate pan, heat a little oil, add slit green chilies, curry leaves, and red chili paste (if using), and saut√© for a few seconds.
  5. Toss the fried chicken in the chili-curry leaf mixture until coated evenly.
  6. Garnish with lemon wedges and serve hot.
